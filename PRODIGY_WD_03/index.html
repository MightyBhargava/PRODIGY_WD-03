Tips to Play

Improve your game with these tips:

Advanced Tips:

  • Always start in the center to maximize your chances of winning.
  • Control the corners to put pressure on your opponent.
  • Try to create multiple threats at once to force your opponent into a losing position.

Game Rules

Here are the official rules for Tic-Tac-Toe:

  • Players take turns marking a square.
  • The first to align three of their marks (either horizontally, vertically, or diagonally) wins.
  • If all squares are filled and no player has aligned three marks, the game ends in a draw.
